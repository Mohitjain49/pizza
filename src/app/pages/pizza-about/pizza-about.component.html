Our Pizzas

At Group Five's Pizza Shop, we've made ordering your pizza simple and seamless. Start by browsing our menu, where you can explore a variety of crusts, toppings, and beverage options to customize your perfect meal. Once you've made your selection, place your order by specifying your preferences for toppings, crust type, and the desired quantity of each menu item. Provide your name, phone number, and address for delivery or pick-up; if you're a returning customer, our system will quickly retrieve your saved details. Finalize your order by filling in your card information. Whether you choose delivery or pick-up, you can count on receiving your fresh, delicious pizza promptly and hassle-free.

Creating An Account

Getting started with Group Five's Pizza Shop is easy! To create an account, simply provide your basic information, including your name, phone number, and address. This ensures that every time you order, we can quickly retrieve your details for a smooth and hassle-free experience. For delivery, you can also include helpful location details like your subdivision name or the nearest major intersection to make sure your pizza arrives hot and fresh.

When setting up your account, you'll have the option to save your card information so you can breeze through the checkout process in the future. Your account also keeps a record of your orders, making it easy to reorder your favorite pizzas or try something new. With your account, you're all set for fast, convenient, and personalized service every time you order!
